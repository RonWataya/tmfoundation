About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2024, the Triphonia Mpinganjira Foundation is a charity rooted in compassion and driven by a deep commitment to empower the people. We travel across Malawi, reaching those in need with hope, opportunity, and lasting change.

Our Vision

To see every Malawian empowered to rise above poverty and thrive with dignity..

Our Mission

To uplift vulnerable groups—especially youth, women, and the sick—through sustainable empowerment programs, compassionate giving, and community engagement..
